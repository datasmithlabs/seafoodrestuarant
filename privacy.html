Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Seafood Restaurant ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you interact with our TikTok Chatbot and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you message our chatbot on TikTok, we may process:

  • Your TikTok OpenID (a unique identifier provided by TikTok).
  • The text content of your messages.
  • Public profile information you have authorized TikTok to share.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information specifically to:

  • Provide automated responses to your inquiries using AI technology.
  • Improve our chatbot's performance and customer service.
  • Communicate updates regarding Seafood Restaurant.

3. AI Processing

By using this service, you acknowledge that your messages are processed by an AI agent (via n8n and third-party AI models) to generate responses. We do not use your personal data to train these models without explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
